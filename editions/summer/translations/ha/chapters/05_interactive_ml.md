# Koyon Injin Mai Mu'amala

Koyon aiki yana tambaya tambaya mai mai da hankali: da wani kasafin kuɗi, waɗanne misalai ya kamata a yi lakabi? Koyon Injin Mai Mu'amala (IML) yana tambaya tambaya mafi faffada: yadda za a tsara *hulda* gaba ɗaya tsakanin ɗan adam da tsarin koyo don kasancewa mai amfani ƙwarai, mai dadi, da daidai?

IML ya bambanta ta **nan da nan** da **kai tsaye** na zagayen ra'ayin dan adam da samfuri. Inda ML na gargajiya ya ƙunshi ɗan adam ya mika bayanai kuma ya jira horarwa ta ƙare, IML yana ba da damar ɗan adam su lura da ɗabi'ar samfuri, ba da ra'ayi, kuma su ga samfuri ya amsa — galibi a cikin daƙiƙa kaɗan.

---

## Ka'idojin Koyon Injin Mai Mu'amala

Amershi et al. {cite}`amershi2014power` suna gano halaye uku na bayyana na IML:

**1. Ra'ayin Mai Sauri:** Samfuri yana sabunta da sauri sosai har ɗan adam ya iya gane tasirin ra'ayinsu. A iyaka, sabuntawar samfuri yana faruwa a lokaci na gaske.

**2. Sarrafa Kai Tsaye:** Ɗan adam yana hulda da samfuri ta bayanan ko ta hasashen samfuri — ba ta fayilolin tsarin ko daidaita abubuwan sama ba.

**3. Gyarawa Mai Sake Zagaye:** Tsari yana mai sake zagaye gaske: aikin ɗan adam na gaba an sanar da shi ta ɗabi'ar samfuri ta yanzu, wanda ya kasance ta ayyukan ɗan adam na baya.

Wannan yana ƙirƙira **zagayen mabiya haɗin gwiwa** mai ɗauri: duka ɗan adam da samfuri suna canzawa akan lokaci don amsa wa juna. Ɗan adam yana koyon abin da samfuri ya fahimta; samfuri yana koyon abin da ɗan adam ya damu da shi.

---

## Kwatancen da Koyon Aiki

IML da koyon aiki suna haɗawa sosai amma ba ɗaya ba ne:

| Dukiya                      | Koyon Aiki                    | ML Mai Mu'amala                  |
|-----------------------------|-------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| Tambayar farko              | Me za a yi lakabi?            | Yadda za a yi mu'amala?          |
| Latency na ra'ayi           | Na iya kasancewa jeri (kwanaki) | Galibi lokaci na gaske ko kusan  |
| Yawan sabuntar samfuri      | Kowace zagaye (jeri)          | Kowace mu'amala (yanar gizo)     |
| Ƙarfin ɗan adam             | Yana amsa tambayoyin samfuri  | Zai iya koyar da samfuri da gangan|
| Zanen mai amfani            | Damuwa ta biyu                | Damuwa ta tsakiya                |
| Nauyin kwamitin tunani na ɗan adam | Ba a ƙiyana ta fili ba | An la'akari da fili              |

A cikin koyon aiki, injin yana kula da mu'amala. A cikin IML, ɗan adam na iya ɗaukar shugabanci kuma — yana ba da misalai, gyarawa, ko ra'ayi akan duk wane gefen ɗabi'ar samfuri wanda ya zama matsala.

---

## Mu'amala ta Shugabanci Hadaye

Tsarin **shugabanci hadaye** yana ba da damar duka ɗan adam da injin su ɗauki shugabanci a lokuta daban-daban {cite}`allen1999mixed`. Tsarin da injin ya kula da shi gaba ɗaya yana tambaya tambayoyi kuma ɗan adam yana amsa. Tsarin da ɗan adam ya kula da shi gaba ɗaya yana barin ɗan adam ya yanke duk komai. Tsarin shugabanci hadaye yana daidaita duka biyun.

A aiki, mafi kyau na tsarin IML suna haɗa:
- **Shugabancin injin:** "Ba ni da tabbas game da waɗannan misalai — za ka iya yin lakabi ga su?"
- **Shugabancin ɗan adam:** "Na lura samfuri koyaushe yana kuskure game da wannan rukuni — bari in ba da misalai fiye da kima"
- **Tabbatarwa:** Samfuri yana fito da fahimtarsa ta yanzu; ɗan adam yana tabbatar ko yin gyarawa

Tsarin IML mai kyau yana mai da fahimtar samfuri ta yanzu a bayyane kuma za a iya gyara shi. Wannan shine buƙatar **fahimtar**: ɗan adam kawai zai iya kula da samfuri da suke fahimta, a ƙalla kusan kusan.

---

## Abubuwan Dan Adam a IML

IML yana kawo abubuwan dan adam — nauyin kwamitin tunani, gajiya, daidaito, da amana — kai tsaye cikin zagayen koyo. Zanen IML mara kyau yana haifar da:

**Gajiyar lakabi:** Ɗan adam suna yanke shawara da sauri, mara kyau yayin da zaman ke tsawaita. Kurakurai da ke shiga bayanin horarwa.

**Nuna son zuciya na anchoring:** Ɗan adam suna dogara sosai akan shawarwarin samfuri na yanzu. Idan mai amfani da hannu ya cika hasashen samfuri, masu sanya lakabi sun fi ƙarancin gyara shi ko da lokacin da bai daidai ba — tushen tsari na ƙurar lakabi wanda ke haɗawa cikin zagaye na lakabi {cite}`geva2019annotator`. Pre-annotation na iya hanzarta gudu {cite}`lingren2014evaluating` yayin da a lokaci ɗaya yana rage yawan da masu sanya lakabi ke kama kurakuran samfuri; waɗannan tasiri biyu dole ne a aunata su da juna a zanen mai amfani na IML.

**Rashin daidaitan amana:** Ɗan adam ko suna amana sosai (suna yarda da sakamakon samfuri mara daidai) ko ba sa amana (suna watsi da shawarwari daidai). Salon biyu suna rage ƙimar haɗin gwiwar dan adam da samfuri.

**Daidaito na zama:** Ɗan adam na iya yanke shawara daban-daban akan misali ɗaya a lokuta daban-daban, musamman bayan zaman mai tsawo. Duba daidaito (sake gabatarwa da misalai na baya) zai iya gano da gyara wannan.

Zanen IML mai kyau yana rage waɗannan matsaloli ta zaɓuɓɓukan mai amfani: gabatar da amincewar samfuri a fili, bazata tsarin gabatarwa, iyakanta tsawon zaman, da gina cikin duba daidaito.

## Nau'in Ra'ayin IML a Aiki

### Ra'ayin Matakin Misali

Ɗan adam yana ba da lakabi ko gyarawa don misali musamman. Wannan mafi yawaitar nau'in ne kuma yana kai tsaye dacewa da koyon supervised.

### Ra'ayin Matakin Fasali

Ɗan adam yana nuna waɗanne fasali suke da dacewa ko mara dacewa. "Samfuri ya kamata ya kula da kalmomi 'gaggawa' da 'lokaci ƙarshe' don wannan rukuni." Wannan ya fi bayyananne fiye da ra'ayin matakin misali kuma na iya zama mai inganci fiye da kima don ayyuka musamman.

Tsarin **TFIDF Interactive** da makamancin su suna ba da damar masu sanya lakabi su haskaka kalmomi masu dacewa a cikin takardun rubutu. Waɗannan fastoci ana canza su zuwa ƙuntatawa ko ƙarin kulawa akan kulawun samfuri.

### Ra'ayin Matakin Samfuri

Ɗan adam yana kai tsaye gyara ɗabi'ar samfuri akan ajin shigarwa: "Duk lokacin da shigarwa ta ƙunshi [X], sakamakon ya kamata ya zama [Y]." Wannan yana yin daidai da ƙa'idojin logic ko ƙuntatawa a cikin hanyoyi kamar Posterior Regularization {cite}`ganchev2010posterior` ko koyon bisa ƙuntatawa.

## Nazarin Yanayi: Google Teachable Machine

Teachable Machine shine tsarin IML na yanar gizo mai sauƙin samun damar wanda ke ba da damar masu amfani marasa fasaha su horar da masu rarraba hotuna a cikin mai bincike. Mai amfani:

1. Yana ɗaukar misalai na kowane aji ta amfani da kameran su
2. Yana horar da samfuri tare da danna ɗaya (daidaita MobileNet a cikin mai bincike)
3. Nan da nan yana ganin hasashen samfuri akan bidiyo mai rai
4. Yana ƙara misalai fiye da kima don aji inda samfuri ba daidai ba

Wannan yana nuna zagayen IML na asali: ba da misalai → lura da samfuri → gano gazawa → ba da misalai mafi mai nufin fiye da kima. Ra'ayin lokaci na gaske (sakamakon samfuri yana sabunta a lokaci na gaske, galibi a yawan firam mai mu'amala akan kayan aikin zamani) yana sa zagayen haɗin gwiwa ya zama kai tsaye da hankali.

## Aiwatar da Sauƙin Zagayen IML

```{code-cell} python
import numpy as np
from sklearn.linear_model import SGDClassifier
from sklearn.preprocessing import StandardScaler
import warnings
warnings.filterwarnings('ignore')

class SimpleIMLSystem:
    """
    Minimal IML system that allows online feedback and displays
    model state after each annotation.
    """

    def __init__(self, n_features=10, n_classes=2):
        self.model = SGDClassifier(loss='log_loss', max_iter=1, warm_start=True,
                                   random_state=42)
        self.scaler = StandardScaler()
        self.X_seen = []
        self.y_seen = []
        self.n_classes = n_classes
        self.initialized = False

    def update(self, x, y_true):
        """Receive a single labeled example and update the model."""
        self.X_seen.append(x)
        self.y_seen.append(y_true)

        if len(self.X_seen) >= 2 * self.n_classes:
            X_arr = np.array(self.X_seen)
            y_arr = np.array(self.y_seen)
            X_scaled = self.scaler.fit_transform(X_arr)
            self.model.partial_fit(X_scaled[-1:], y_arr[-1:],
                                   classes=list(range(self.n_classes)))
            self.initialized = True

        return self

    def predict_with_confidence(self, x):
        """Predict label and return confidence."""
        if not self.initialized:
            return None, 0.0
        x_scaled = self.scaler.transform(x.reshape(1, -1))
        probs = self.model.predict_proba(x_scaled)[0]
        return self.model.predict(x_scaled)[0], probs.max()

    def current_accuracy(self, X_val, y_val):
        if not self.initialized:
            return None
        X_scaled = self.scaler.transform(X_val)
        return (self.model.predict(X_scaled) == y_val).mean()


# Simulate an IML session
rng = np.random.default_rng(42)
X_all, y_all = np.random.default_rng(0).random((500, 10)), np.random.default_rng(0).integers(0,2,500)
X_val, y_val = X_all[400:], y_all[400:]

system = SimpleIMLSystem(n_features=10, n_classes=2)
accs, confidences = [], []

print("Step | Labels | Accuracy | Example confidence")
print("-" * 50)

for step in range(100):
    x, y = X_all[step], y_all[step]
    pred, conf = system.predict_with_confidence(x)
    system.update(x, y)

    if (step + 1) % 10 == 0:
        acc = system.current_accuracy(X_val, y_val)
        if acc is not None:
            accs.append(acc)
            print(f"  {step+1:3d} |  {step+1:4d}  |  {acc:.3f}   | {conf:.3f}")
```

## Jarabawar Kaka

Wata hanyar da ke da amfani don kimanta zanen mai amfani na IML — kuma zanen tsarin HITL gaba ɗaya — shine abin da za mu kira **Jarabawar Kaka** (asalin kiraye da aka gabatar a nan a matsayin ƙuntatawa na zane, ba a matsayin nuni ga aikin baya ba):

> *Mace da aka haifa a 1930 ya kamata ta iya amfani da wannan na'ura ta murya, kuma idan ta ji damuwa, ta ragewa mai kyau zuwa mai amfani da haɗin maɓalli ko mai amfani da rubutu.*

Jarabawa ba ta farko game da isa ba, ko da yake haka ne ma. Ita game da **zane don gogayya** ne. Idan tsari yana buƙatar ƙirar cibiyar jijiya, zagayen horarwa, ko rarrabawar iya yiwuwa don amfani da shi yadda ya dace, ya gaza Jarabawar Kaka. Ɗan adam a cikin zagaye ya kamata ya iya shiga ba tare da fahimtar gefen injin na zagaye ba.

Abubuwa masu amfani don zanen IML suna da ƙwanƙwasa:

**Makoma ta farko ta murya:** Yanayin mu'amala na farko ya kamata ya zama harshen yau da kullum ko motsi — ba masu juyawa na sinadarai ko iyakar tabbatarwa ba. Ƙwararru na iya son masu juyawa; kowa ya kamata ya iya cewa "ba daidai ba ne."

**Lalacewa mai kyau:** Lokacin da yanayin mai amfani da shi ya fifita ya gaza ko ya haifar da damuwa, tsari ya kamata ya ba da wata hanya — ba allon fanko ko saƙon kuskure ba. Mai amfani da shi wanda ba zai iya hulda ba ba zai iya koyar da shi ba.

**Halin samfuri mai bayyana:** Ya kamata a ga fahimtar yanzu ta samfuri a cikin kalmomin dan adam. Ba "amincewar: 0.73" amma "Na tabbata sosai wannan [X] ne, amma na ga misalai kamar wannan suka je hanyoyi biyu." Ya kamata rashin tabbas ya kasance a cikin harshe wanda ke gayyata gyarawa.

**Juriya ga rikitarwa:** Mai amfani da shekara 93 da injiniyan ML mai shekara 23 za su yi mu'amala daban-daban da tsari ɗaya. Jarabawar Kaka na tambaya ko tsari zai iya karɓa duka biyun — ba ta gano shekarun mai amfani ba, amma ta tsara mu'amala waɗanda ke aiki akan iyakar ƙwarewar da jin daɗin.

Jarabawa tana ɗaukar mahimmanci musamman yayin da tsarin ML ke motsi daga kayan aiki na bincike zuwa ababen more rayuwa na yau da kullum. Tsarin tallafi na hoto likitanci da likitan hoto ke amfani da shi, mai rarraba takardun doka da ma'aikata ke amfani da shi, tsarin ra'ayin ilimi da malamai ke amfani da shi — kowannensu yana haɗa dan adam a cikin zagaye waɗanda ba su yi rajista don kasancewa mai horar da AI ba. Zane don su ba kyautar wani ba ne; shi ne manufar.

:::{admonition} Ka'idar Zane
:class: tip
Jarabawar Kaka ƙuntatawa ce ta zane, ba rukunin da ake nufin ba. Tsarin da suke wucewa sun fi ƙarfi ga iri-iri na mai amfani, sun fi sadaukarwa ga tazara na ƙwarewar, kuma suna da gaskiya fiye da kima game da abin da suke buƙata daga dan adam a cikin zagaye. Idan tsari yana buƙatar bayani kafin amfani, yana roƙon ɗan adam ya yi aiki ƙari. Ya kamata a tabbatar da wannan aiki ta fa'ida kwatankwacin shi.
:::

## IML da Samfurin Ginshiƙai

ML mai mu'amala na zamani yana dogara fiye da kima akan **samfurin ginshiƙai da aka horar a baya** {cite}`bommasani2021opportunities` a matsayin tushe. Maimakon horarwa daga farko, masu amfani suna daidaita babban samfuri da aka horar a baya tare da ƙaramin adadin misalai na mu'amala. Wannan zai iya rage yawan misalai da ake buƙata don kaiwa aiki mai amfani sosai — a yanayi mafi kyau, kaɗan kamar misalai 5–50 maimakon dubbai, dangane da yadda embeddings na baya ya dace da aikin manufa {cite}`bommasani2021opportunities`.

Dabarun da ke ba da damar wannan sun haɗa:
- **Tambayar kaɗan-kaɗan:** Bayar da misalai a cikin tagar mahallin LLM
- **Daidaita adapter:** Sabunta ƙananan modules na adapter yayin daskarewa da tushe
- **Daidaita sinadarai mai inganci (PEFT):** LoRA, daidaita prefix, da makamancin hanyoyin da ke ba da damar sabuntar da sauri, mara albarkatu

Samfurin ginshiƙai suna canza yanayin IML: ɗan adam ba su ƙara koyar da samfuri daga farko amma *kula da* samfuri wanda ya riga ya san abubuwa da yawa. Kalubale yana canzawa daga "yadda za a ba da misalai masu isa" zuwa "yadda za a ƙayyadewa ainihin abin da muke son ya bambanta daga abin da samfuri ya riga ya yi."
