Lazaros Toumanidis conceived, directed, and edited \emph{Human in the Loop: Misunderstood}---but he did not write it alone, or, in the traditional sense, write it at all. The text was produced in collaboration with a range of AI tools: drafted, expanded, and refined through human-in-the-loop iteration, with human curation, fact-checking, and correction throughout.

\medskip

The book is, in that sense, its own example---made the way it argues good systems should work: not a machine acting alone, not a human doing everything by hand, but a person and their tools each doing what they do best, with a human in the loop who stays accountable for the result.

\medskip

This project brings together the Winter textbook, the Summer reading edition, the Autumn pamphlet, the Spring build edition, and the companion materials that accompany them.
